Population Density of Hispanic People in the Contiguous United States

Problem

Illustrate the spatial distribution of the Hispanic population in the contiguous U.S. by state, according to the 2010 U.S. Census, with a Choropleth map.

Solution

Importance

Analyzing demographic data, such as the density of the Hispanic population by state, is crucial to help identify the needs of local populations. For example, this data can be leveraged by policymakers to determine which languages to use for program information and by entrepreneurs to decide on the type of restaurant to open. Considering demographics supports the provision of culturally competent services and aids in developing thriving communities.
