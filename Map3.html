Pennsylvania 2020 Presidential Election Results

Problem

Map the election results from the 2020 Pennsylvania Presidential Election by county.

Solution

Counties with darker colored blues represent more Democratic areas and counties with darker colored reds represent Republic areas.

Importance

Analyzing election results by party can be useful for a variety of reasons: campaign strategists may look for areas they can make potential future gains or need to shore up support to prevent losses and it can give a high level understanding about the priorities and values of the residents. The maps themselves can be used to effectively communicate results to residents in ways that highlight transparency and build confidence in election results.

Selected Skills

  1. Election Maps and Analysis Coding election results by party by county to ensure easy analysis.

    To effectively communicate election data through a map, I separated the Republican and Democratic counties into new spatial layers through selecting by attributes and exporting the selected data. This allowed me to color code each dataset with a separate color and manually select a custom gradient for each.

  2. Critical Thinking Building on GIS tools tools already learned in new ways and problem solving to determine how to solve new problem types with little direct instruction.

    This was a challenging problem because although I had learned all the tools needed to create an election map with two colors, instruction was not provided on how to separate the map into the two-color schemes. Critical thinking was required to identify which tools that I had previously learned would be helpful and brainstorming new ways to apply them.

  3. Visual Esthetics Communicating findings in a manner that is not only easy to understand but is attractive to the reader.

    A key aspect of creating effective maps is to engage the audience visually, beyond merely presenting the relevant information. To enhance contrast and readability, I intentionally modified the default color schemes, ensuring that the map is both visually appealing and easy to understand.
